Welcome

Today we will learn the vowels in English.

A E I O U

Vowels are special letters. They are in many words we read and say every day.

Lesson Goals

  • Identify the vowels: A, E, I, O, U
  • Say the vowel sounds clearly
  • Find vowels in simple words
  • Choose the correct vowel in a word
